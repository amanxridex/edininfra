Friends Colony – Premium Plots near Galgotias University
Brochure

Friends Colony by ED Infratech is a premium plotted development located in Sector 17A, Dankaur. Enjoying a strategic location near Galgotias University, this project offers an investment-friendly environment with wide internal roads, planned layout, and a green environment.

With a pre-launch offer of up to ₹1 Lakh discount, plots are available at just ₹28,000 per sq. yard. Secure your freehold plot with a low booking amount of ₹11,000 and capitalize on the rapid infrastructure development in the Yamuna Authority region.
